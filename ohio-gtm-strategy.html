Did WHOOP Miss the Ohio Deadline? Honest Answer.
The two Ohio solicitations with passed or approaching deadlines are both services contracts — workforce training and CHW training programs. WHOOP is not a workforce training provider. WHOOP does not submit to these RFPs. So the answer is: no, WHOOP did not miss a WHOOP-specific procurement window.

What matters is this: Ohio's $92M Innovation Hubs initiative — the largest and most WHOOP-relevant piece — has not issued an RFP yet. The pre-market window is open right now. The organizations who have conversations with ODH before that solicitation drops will be in the requirements document. That is the window you're in, and it is not closed.
The June 15 Action — CHW Training Partner Play
The DOH59679 CHW Training solicitation closes June 15, 2026. WHOOP doesn't bid directly, but organizations submitting proposals to run Ohio's CHW training program are building technology ecosystems around that program. A CHW who monitors patients with WHOOP biometric data (HRV, resting heart rate, SpO2, sleep) has a dramatically more powerful chronic disease management tool than one without it. Contact the likely prime bidders — public health consultancies, academic health centers, and rural health workforce organizations — and ask to be named as the consumer monitoring tool in their CHW training curriculum. This is the Montana model applied to Ohio.
