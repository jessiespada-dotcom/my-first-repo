How to use this list: Hospital Districts (green) are the Initiative 1 Part 1 direct award targets — government-owned, highest priority for WHOOP outreach. All Rural Hospitals (blue) qualify for Initiative 1 Part 2 competitive awards. All hospitals in this list are also potential targets for Initiative 4 (workforce wellness) and Initiative 6 (capital equipment). This list is based on state records and 2020 Census data — confirm the official HHSC direct award list with Anthony Hawley (anthony.hawley@hhs.texas.gov).
