WHOOP Honest Assessment — Where the Real Opportunity Is
The two active Ohio solicitations (DOH59715 Workforce — closed March 10; DOH59679 CHW Training — closes June 15) are services contracts, not technology device procurement. WHOOP does not submit to workforce training or CHW training services RFPs directly. However, the Innovation Hubs initiative ($92M) — Ohio's largest piece — has not released an RFP yet. That is where remote patient monitoring, consumer health technology, and care coordination tools will be procured. The pre-market window to shape those requirements is open right now. Engage ODH about the technology and RPM component of Innovation Hubs before any solicitation drops.
