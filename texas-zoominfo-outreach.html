How to Use in ZoomInfo
1
Search by Company
In ZoomInfo, go to Company Search and type the hospital name exactly as shown. For small rural hospitals, try variations — some may appear under a parent health system name or slightly different spelling.
2
Filter by Role
Use the Contact filter within the company to find the roles listed. Start with the green (Primary) role first. If not found, move to blue (Clinical), then orange (Program).
3
Log in Salesforce / CRM
Once found, export the contact or log it directly. For small rural hospital districts, the CEO or Administrator is often also the budget owner — prioritize them above all other titles.
Role priority for WHOOP: At small rural hospital districts (most under 50 beds), the CEO / Hospital Administrator is almost always the budget decision maker AND the person who submits the HHSC scope of work. They are your #1 contact. The CMO / Medical Director is your clinical champion — they care most about outcomes. The Director of Community Health or Population Health is the program owner who will actually run a WHOOP initiative day-to-day. If you can only find one contact, find the CEO.
